Entrance "Emerge" = the reference's read: particles surface
  in place from the background (fade + scale, no travel); Static skips it,
  Settle springs from a few px. Depth scales particle size and brightness by
  a per-particle z; float adds volumetric drift; parallax shifts the cloud
  with the cursor by depth — the three together are the "3D" feel.
  CLICK drops a stone in water: a main ripple plus two echoes travel outward
  at waveSpeed and the spring re-forms the mark behind them. When it feels
  right, Copy config.
